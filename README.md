## The Problem

We've all been there:
- Two people buy the same gift for someone's birthday
- You want to give a great gift but don't know what they want
- Coordinating with family and friends about who's buying what becomes a messy group chat
- The birthday person has to pretend they're excited about their third coffee maker

**Gift-giving should bring joy, not coordination headaches.**

## The Solution

Wishlist Manager is a straightforward wishlist application that solves gift duplication through a simple reservation system. Create wishlists for different events, share them with friends and family, and let people reserve gifts - ensuring everyone gets exactly what they want, with no duplicates.

## ğŸ“± How It Works

### For Wishlist Creators:

1. **Sign Up** - Create your account
2. **Create an Event** - Add your birthday, wedding, or any celebration
3. **Add Gifts** - Build your wishlist with items you'd love to receive
4. **Share the Link** - Send the unique event link to friends and family
5. **Watch It Fill Up** - See gifts get reserved as people choose what to give

### For Gift Givers:

1. **Open the Link** - Click the wishlist link you received
2. **Browse** - Look through available gifts
3. **Reserve** - Click "Reserve" on the gift you want to give
4. **Done!** - The gift is marked as taken, preventing duplicates
